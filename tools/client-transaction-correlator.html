Client Transaction Correlator

Correlates card transaction data with client zip codes from SimplePractice exports — so you can quickly see where your revenue is coming from geographically.

What it does

SimplePractice lets you export transaction records and client data separately. This app takes both exports and produces a combined view that matches payments to client zip codes — useful for understanding your practice's geographic reach, analyzing sliding scale distribution, or preparing reports.

How to use it

  1. Export from SimplePractice — go to Reports and export your transaction data and client list as CSV files.
  2. Open the app — drag both CSV files into the app window.
  3. Run the correlation — the app matches transactions to clients by ID and outputs a combined CSV.
  4. Review the output — open the result in Excel or Numbers.

Privacy

All processing happens locally on your machine. No data is sent anywhere. The app does not require an internet connection.
